So, who am I?

Hello,

I'm Lisa, a digital design student at Kristianstad University, pursuing a degree in Digital Design. My studies have provided me with a solid foundation in creating innovative, user-centered designs that balance aesthetics and functionality. Through hands-on projects and practical learning, I’ve developed expertise in UI/UX design, graphic design, and web design, with skills spanning both frontend and backend development to deliver seamless digital solutions. I have gained extensive experience using Adobe programs, Figma, and Canva, which have enhanced my ability to design and prototype intuitive user interfaces, create visually compelling graphics, and develop cohesive brand identities. Creativity is at the core of my work, and I express it not only through design but also through painting.
