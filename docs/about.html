About.

So, who am I?

Hello,

I’m Lisa, a digital designer with a degree in Digital Design from Kristianstad University. My education has equipped me with a strong foundation in creating innovative, user-centered designs, emphasizing both aesthetics and functionality. Kristianstad University’s focus on practical, real-world skills has shaped my expertise in UI/UX design, graphic design, and web design, allowing me to work across both frontend and backend development to deliver seamless digital solutions. Creativity is at the core of who I am—not just in design but also in my personal life, where I express myself through painting. When I’m not designing or creating art, I enjoy spending time with friends and family, exploring new places, and savoring delicious food. At home, you’ll often find me cuddling with my two small poodles, who bring endless joy to my days. For me, life is about balancing creativity, connection, and a passion for meaningful experiences.
