About.

So, who am I?

Hello,

I'm Lisa, a digital design student at Kristianstad University, pursuing a degree in Digital Design. My studies have provided me with a solid foundation in creating innovative, user-centered designs that balance aesthetics and functionality. Through hands-on projects and practical learning, I’ve developed expertise in UI/UX design, graphic design, and web design, with skills spanning both frontend and backend development to deliver seamless digital solutions. Creativity is at the core of who I am—not just in design but also in my personal life, where I express myself through painting. When I’m not designing or creating art, I enjoy spending time with friends and family, exploring new places, and savoring delicious food. At home, you’ll often find me cuddling with my two small poodles, who bring endless joy to my days. For me, life is about balancing creativity, connection, and a passion for meaningful experiences.
