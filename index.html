As a digital designer, my strategy is rooted in creating user-centered solutions that balance aesthetics with functionality. Recent projects

Winte time

2024

An application to enhance the wine experience by tailoring recommendations to individual preferences. Users can rate wines they’ve enjoyed, and the app uses this data to suggest new wines that align with their tastes, helping them explore new options with confidence. The goal was to create a seamless and intuitive experience that allows wine enthusiasts to discover new favorites and enjoy the perfect glass every time.

Señor Savant

2024

Señor Savant is a branding project that involved creating a complete visual identity for the company. The project included the design of a logo, selection of color schemes, typography, and the development of various promotional materials. The goal was to craft a modern and sophisticated brand that captures the essence of the company while appealing to its target audience. The visual identity was carefully designed to ensure consistency across all touchpoints, delivering a strong and memorable brand presence.

Pluggportalen

2024

Pluggportalen is a website designed to help students connect with like-minded individuals for collaborative studying. The platform provides a space for students to share resources, ask questions, and find study partners based on similar academic interests. The design focused on creating a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to navigate and connect with others. By fostering a supportive online community, Pluggportalen aims to enhance the learning experience and encourage collaboration among students.
